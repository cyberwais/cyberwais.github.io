PROJECT CYBERWAIS

ABOUT

CYBERWAIS is a campaign for protection, awareness, and responsibility in the digital world.

This project promotes people to stay safe online. We want everyone to learn how to protect their personal information and how to spot digital threats like scams or hackers. Being safe online is not just about knowing the rules; it is about making safety a part of what you do every day. Our goal is to make cybersecurity easy for everyone to understand and use.

WHY CYBERWAIS?

The name is heavily inspired from popular E-sports game player named "Wise" who is often called as "Wais" and is known for his strategic gameplays.

CYBERWAIS TIPS FOR TODAY

Small habits lead to the essence of security. We can start with these today:

🔒 Password Power

Use long passwords with a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols. Avoid using "123456" or your birthday.

🔎 Link Check

Before clicking a link in an email or message, hover over it to see where it actually leads. Don't trust suspicious senders.

📲 App Permissions

Check your phone settings. If a simple calculator app asks for your location or contacts, deny it.

💻 Software Updates

Always update your apps and phone system. These updates often fix "holes" that hackers use to get in.

Stay tuned for more updates in the future!
